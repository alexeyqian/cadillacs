The Golden Ratio for Beat 'Em Ups
The most balanced foundation for a standard 1080p screen (1920x1080 pixels) uses a 1:2 character-to-lane ratio:
* Character Height: 160 to 200 pixels.
* Character Width: 60 to 80 pixels.
* Total Walkable Floor (Z-Axis): 320 to 400 pixels deep.

To handle 2.5D positioning properly, movement collision uses a completely separate box from combat hitboxes. This is the "Shadow Box" resting at the character's feet.
Two characters cannot occupy the same physical space on the dirt floor.
* Standard Footprint Box: 32px wide × 10px tall.
* Heavy Footprint Box: 48px wide × 14px tall.
* The Rule of Overlap: If Player A's footprint box collides with Enemy B's footprint box, the physics engine pushes them apart. This prevents sprites from awkwardly blending into one another.
